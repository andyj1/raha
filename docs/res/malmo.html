ECCV 2026 Itinerary

Malmö, Sweden & Copenhagen, Denmark

🇸🇪 Välkommen! (Welcome!)

This itinerary is tightly optimized to balance your workshop commitments, your poster presentation, and exploring the best of both Malmö and Copenhagen. Use the interactive map below to orient yourself between the Malmö Arena conference site and your sightseeing destinations.

Day 1: Arrival & Orientation

Mon, Sept 7
Afternoon
  • Transit 2:00 PM – 4:00 PM: Arrive at Copenhagen Airport (CPH).
    • Take the Øresundståg train directly across the famous Øresund bridge to Malmö.
  • Logistics 4:30 PM – 6:30 PM: Check into your hotel in Malmö and collect early ECCV registration materials.
Evening
  • Sightseeing 7:00 PM – 9:00 PM: Explore Lilla torg (Little Square).
    • Enjoy your first Swedish meal at a terrace restaurant in this historic 16th-century cobblestone square.

Day 2: Workshops & Architecture

Tue, Sept 8
Daytime
  • Conference 8:30 AM – 5:00 PM: Workshops at Malmö Arena (Hyllie district).
Evening
  • Sightseeing 5:30 PM – 7:00 PM: Visit the Turning Torso in Västra Hamnen (Western Harbour).
    • Walk the waterfront promenade and admire Scandinavia's tallest, twisted skyscraper.
  • Dining 7:30 PM – 9:00 PM: Seafood dinner along the Western Harbour boardwalk watching the sunset over the strait.

Day 3: Workshops & History

Wed, Sept 9
Daytime
  • Conference 8:30 AM – 5:00 PM: Workshops and Expo at MalmöMässan.
Evening
  • Sightseeing 5:30 PM – 7:30 PM: Evening stroll around Malmö Castle (Malmöhus).
    • Walk through the peaceful Slottsparken (Castle Park) and along the historic moat.
  • Dining 8:00 PM – 9:30 PM: Dinner in the Gamla Staden (Old Town) district.

Day 4: Main Conference & Swedish Traditions

Thu, Sept 10
Daytime
  • Conference 9:00 AM – 5:00 PM: ECCV Main Conference Keynotes and Oral Sessions.
Evening
  • Experience 6:00 PM – 8:00 PM: Ribersborgs open-air bath (Ribersborgs Kallbadhus).
    • Recharge before your presentation with a traditional Swedish wood-fired sauna and an ocean plunge.
  • Dining 8:30 PM – 10:00 PM: Casual dinner in the Ribersborg neighborhood.

Day 5: Poster Presentation Day

Fri, Sept 11
Morning
  • Prep 8:00 AM – 9:00 AM: Pin up poster at MalmöMässan and review talking points.
  • Conference 9:00 AM – 12:00 PM: Morning conference sessions.
Afternoon & Evening
  • Presentation 1:00 PM – 4:00 PM: Poster Presentation Session. Engage with peers and showcase research.
  • Social 5:00 PM – 7:00 PM: Official ECCV 2026 Social Event / Gala Dinner.
  • Celebrate 7:30 PM onward: Post-presentation drinks with lab mates at a local pub in central Malmö.

Day 6: Conclusion & Copenhagen

Sat, Sept 12
Morning
  • Conference 9:00 AM – 1:00 PM: Final ECCV sessions and closing remarks.
  • Transit 1:30 PM – 2:30 PM: Grab luggage and take the train across the bridge to Copenhagen, Denmark.
Afternoon & Evening
  • Sightseeing 3:30 PM – 5:30 PM: Explore Nyhavn.
    • Photograph the iconic colorful townhouses and historic ships. Grab a Danish coffee.
  • Sightseeing 5:30 PM – 8:30 PM: Wander the Indre By (Inner City) district.
  • Dining 8:30 PM: Celebratory dinner at a lively restaurant along the Copenhagen canals.

Day 7: Departure Day

Sun, Sept 13
Morning
  • Sightseeing 9:00 AM – 12:00 PM: Visit Tivoli Gardens (right next to Copenhagen Central Station).
    • Wander through the beautifully landscaped 19th-century amusement park.
    • Grab a quick Danish pastry before heading to the airport.
  • Transit Afternoon: Short train ride from Copenhagen Central to CPH Airport for departure.
